02

About This App

NoLootLeftBehind is a game price comparison tool that helps users find and compare prices for video games across different platforms and stores. The App does not require user account creation or login.

03

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details.

Automatically Collected Information

To function properly, the App may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device information (operating system version, device type)
  • App usage data (features accessed, search queries entered within the App)
  • Network state information (to check internet connectivity)

Permissions Used

The App requests the following Android permissions:

INTERNETRequired to fetch game price data from online sources.
NETWORK_STATEUsed to check whether an internet connection is available before making network requests.
NOTIFICATIONSUsed to send you optional notifications (e.g., price drop alerts, if enabled).
STORAGEUsed to store app data or cache locally on your device (limited to older Android versions as required).
BOOT_COMPLETEDUsed to restart scheduled notifications after your device reboots.
ALERT_WINDOWMay be used to display overlay content on supported devices.
VIBRATE / WAKEUsed to support notification delivery.
